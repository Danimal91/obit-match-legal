# Obit Match — Support

Need help with Obit Match, have a question, or want to report an issue? We're happy to help.

**Contact us:** [obitmatchcontact@gmail.com](mailto:obitmatchcontact@gmail.com)

We aim to respond within a few business days. When reaching out, it helps to include:

- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Your device type and iOS version, if the issue is technical

## Common questions

**How do I delete my account and data?**
Email us at the address above and we'll complete the deletion within 30 days, as described in our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.html).

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed through Apple, not us: open Settings on your device → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Obit Match → Cancel Subscription.

**Where do obituaries come from?**
See "Where obituary information comes from" in our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.html).
