# Obit Match — Privacy Policy

**DRAFT — for attorney review before publication. Last updated: July 11, 2026**

Obit Match ("we," "us") helps you learn, respectfully and privately, when someone you may have known has passed away. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

## Information you give us

When you fill out your profile, you may provide:

- Your name and email address
- Your year of birth
- Family surnames (your own, maiden name, parents', grandparents')
- Hometown (city and state)
- Schools attended, employers, and military service, with approximate years

Every profile field except name and email is optional. The more you share, the better we can find meaningful connections — but you choose what to share.

## How we use your information

We use your profile for **one purpose**: comparing it against recently published obituaries to suggest people you may have known. We do not sell your information, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose unrelated to finding your connections.

Your email address is used only to communicate with you about your account and, if you enable them, notifications about new matches.

## Where obituary information comes from

Obituary information in the app comes from publicly available sources — primarily official feeds and public pages published by funeral homes and newspapers. We store only factual details (names, dates, places, schools, employers, service) extracted from those public sources, and we always link you to the original publisher's website to read the obituary itself.

## What we do NOT do

- We do not sell or rent your personal information.
- We do not show your profile to other users. Matching is private: only you see your matches.
- We do not republish obituary text; we link to the funeral home or newspaper that published it.
- We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Obit Match is intended for adults.

## Storage and security

Your profile and match history are stored on secured servers. We retain your information while your account is active. We take reasonable measures to protect it, but no system is perfectly secure.

## Your choices and rights

- **Edit**: you can update or remove any profile field at any time in the app.
- **Delete**: to delete your profile and all associated data, contact us at the address below and we will complete the deletion within 30 days.
- **Notifications**: match notifications are optional and controlled in your device settings.

Depending on where you live (e.g., California), you may have additional rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us to exercise them.

## For funeral homes and publishers

If you operate a funeral home or publication and prefer that factual details from obituaries you publish not appear in Obit Match, contact us and we will remove your organization as a source promptly — our goal is to honor your wishes the same day.

## Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you in the app before they take effect.
